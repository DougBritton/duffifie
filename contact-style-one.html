  • duffifie| də ’fī fē | - verb(past duffified | - fēd | ; participle duffifying | - fē iNG | )
  • [with obj.]to lay down a bottle on its side for some time after its contents have been poured out, that it may be completely
  • drained of the few drops remaining in it: duffifie that bottle, young lad; there’s still enough for another swig
  • [figurative]I don’t always finish my drink, but when I do, I duffifie.
  • ORIGIN: Scottish (from the Dictionary of the Scots Language), formerly obsolete
